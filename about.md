# About

> "I propose to consider the question, 'Can machines think?'" ~ Alan Turing

I always wondered how artificial intelligence can even be possible. This question quickly led me into the world of data science and machine learning. I realized that AI is essentially powered by statistics and mathematics. In a way, machine learning is "an evolution of statistics", because it builds on statistical concepts to handle complex data problems.

This blog is supposed to be a log book capturing my data science learning journey from the very beginning.
